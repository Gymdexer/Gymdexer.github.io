What’s in it for you?

  • Not only will you be able to find your favorite exercises to do in the gym, you will also be able to train smarter. This combination will make sure that you stay motivated. Why do you want to train smarter? It will save you a lot of time by doing the right things in the right way. This way you will get to your goals sooner and with less to no injuries.
  • If time isn’t an issue, financial efficiency might. Thanks to years of combined research and experience with lifting weights, eating foods, taking supplements and using gear; this website will be able to provide you with the knowledge on all of the above topics. This way we can provide you with the best info for your goals, without you spending too much.

Why Gymdex?

Because Gymdex is a collection of experiences and knowledge of lifters and gym’ers that have found the right sport or gym activity for them. This way we hope to lead you to the right sport or the right combination of exercises for you. If there are any products that get recommended, it means that the lifters who preform the sport in question, uses that product from that specific store him- or herself. This means that the right balance between price and quality will always be applied.

We practice what we preach: If another source or product is more useful or better in quality, the post will be adjusted so that the product mentioned will always be the one that the author would go for. This means that what looks like advertisement, is actually an honest suggestion where authenticity is central. We believe that this authenticity will help the beginning, average and advanced lifter with the right choices, which will make you -and everyone else you share this website with- succeed the best way possible. Besides that, we will try to get some extra discounts on the products that get shown on the website. Because all the little bits help.

What after I have found my sport?

Then you still might consider looking at the other sports to gain some insights in overall training or just to spice up your training by adding in some movements from other activities.

The /Blog section will be updated with a new article every week. The topics will range from basic training info and tips to advanced and scientifically supported research.

What also stays applicable during your whole journey, are the gym-related products: The /Products-page will always be at the right corner of the screen so that you can quickly find extra and new promotions on top of the existing ones. Gymdex will do its very best to keep that page updated so that you can come back to get even more cash off of your favorite products. So don’t forget to check this page before you order anything, anywhere!
